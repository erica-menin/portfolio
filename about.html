A little about me

Passionate about design and human behaviour, I’ve dived into the UX design field after years working as a visual designer.

I’ve always been driven to achieve my goals and I found passion in challenging situations: from working in one of the biggest magazine publishers in Brazil, to developing web design skills while travelling the world.

Getting in contact with people from different backgrounds made me expand my vision about human behaviour and got me where I am today. Now more than ever, I’m fascinated about the whole Design Thinking process.

I truly believe in the human-centered approach which enables me to develop products that go beyond just pretty interfaces.
